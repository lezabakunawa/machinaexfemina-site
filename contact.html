Live now; make now always the most precious time. Now will never come again. If we're going to be damned, let's be damned for what we really are. Insufficient facts always invite danger. Madness has no purpose. Or reason. But it may have a goal. Genius doesn't work on an assembly line basis. Did Einstein, Kazanga or Sitar of Vulcan produce new and revolutionary theories on a regular schedule? You can't simply say, 'Today I will be brilliant.' Improve a mechanical device and you may double productivity. But improve man, you gain a thousandfold.
